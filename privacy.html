Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Information We Collect

PUMP PONIES collects minimal personal information. We prioritize your privacy and only collect what is necessary to operate the betting platform.

Information collected includes:

  • Public wallet addresses (when you send SOL to bet)
  • Betting history and transaction records
  • Browser and device information for analytics
  • IP address for security purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process betting deposits and payouts
  • Match deposits to specific races and horses
  • Display race results and betting statistics
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal requirements

Blockchain Transparency

All betting transactions are recorded on the Solana blockchain, which is publicly accessible. Your wallet address and transaction history can be viewed by anyone using blockchain explorers.

We generate unique deposit addresses for each bet, which provides some privacy for your betting patterns while maintaining blockchain transparency.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Store session information
  • Improve site performance

We may use analytics tools to understand platform usage patterns and improve our services.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to sensitive data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party services including:

  • Solana blockchain network
  • RPC providers for blockchain access
  • Analytics services
  • Pump.fun for live streaming

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your data.

Data Retention

We retain betting records and transaction data for:

  • Operational purposes (processing payouts)
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Dispute resolution

Blockchain data is permanent and cannot be deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of off-chain data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

Note: On-chain blockchain data cannot be deleted or modified.

Children's Privacy

PUMP PONIES is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We will post the updated date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our official Telegram or X (Twitter) channels.
